Notes

  • Solar eclipse exposure and safety are not modeled here. Use certified solar filters except during totality.
  • Lunar eclipse obscuration is global, but visibility still depends on the Moon being above the horizon at your pin.
  • The app uses live astronomy calculations in the browser, so the first update after moving the map may take a second.
